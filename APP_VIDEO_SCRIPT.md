# VisualMoney: Video Showcase Script

This script outlines the flow for a comprehensive application demonstration video, highlighting the journey from a new user to a premium power investor.

---

## Part 1: First Impressions (Onboarding)
**Visual**: App opens to the splash/onboarding screen with vibrant gradients.
*   **Scene**: User enters their name and selects their primary currency.
*   **Narrative**: "Welcome to VisualMoney. Your journey to mastering wealth starts with a seamless onboarding. Tell us who you are, and let's set up your global dashboard."

---

## Part 2: The Command Center (Home Screen)
**Visual**: The main Home Screen fades in, showing total balance and the scrolling market ticker.
*   **Scene**: Smoothly scrolling through the portfolio list.
*   **Narrative**: "This is your financial command center. Get an instant snapshot of your total net worth and daily performance. Clean, fast, and institutional-grade."

---

## Part 3: Growing the Portfolio (Adding Assets)
**Visual**: User clicks the '+' button. Searches for "AAPL" or "BTC".
*   **Scene**: Entering quantity and price for a new asset.
*   **Narrative**: "Adding assets is effortless. Whether it's Stocks, Crypto, or Gold, our direct integration with global market data ensures your tracking is always precise."

---

## Part 4: Managing Time (Calendar & Reminders)
**Visual**: Switching to the Calendar tab.
*   **Scene**: Showing the dots on the calendar representing earnings or dividend events automatically added for the portfolio.
*   **Narrative**: "Never miss a market catalyst. The integrated calendar automatically tracks key events for your holdings, giving you a timeline of what matters."

---

## Part 5: Deep Dive (Asset Details & News)
**Visual**: Clicking on a specific asset (e.g., Apple).
*   **Scene**: Showing the interactive charts, and then scrolling down to the News section. Clicking a news item to show redirection.
*   **Narrative**: "Go beneath the surface. Interactive historical charts show you the trends, while our curated news feed keeps you updated on the latest shifts affecting your specific assets."

---

## Part 6: The Pro Edge (Premium Features)
**Visual**: Clicking 'Go Premium'. Show the Premium Dashboard with the Risk Score and Sector Donut Chart.
*   **Scene**: Highlighting the "Portfolio Pulse" and the "Dividend Outlook".
*   **Narrative**: "For the serious investor, VisualMoney Premium unlocks the pro edge. Track your Portfolio Risk Score, analyze your sector diversification with high-fidelity charts, and project your passive income with our Dividend Outlook."

---

## Closing
**Visual**: Fading out with the VisualMoney logo.
*   **Narrative**: "VisualMoney. Visualize your goals. Master your wealth."
